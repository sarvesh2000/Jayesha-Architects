A Leader in Architecture Design

Jayesha Architects and Interior Designers was established in 1990 in T. Nagar, Chennai by Sathiesh Kumar. In this period we've grown to become a practice that constantly learns from its experiences and sets new benchmarks for itself. Since our inception, we've worked on a range of projects like residences, apartments, showrooms, offices, laboratories, and many more.

We believe our role as architects is to create a space that can elevate the quality of our life - the way we occupy and function in this space. Therefore, our primary focus is on the design content. Our talented team brings their collective skills to the design process in every project we undertake.
